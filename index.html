FAQs

Common questions

Is Test & Tag mandatory?
Test & Tag is required in many workplaces under WHS regulations and Australian Standards, particularly in construction and high-risk environments.
Can anyone do this course?
Yes. No electrical background is required — the course is designed for anyone responsible for inspecting and maintaining portable electrical equipment.
Does this qualify me as an electrician?
No. This course allows you to test and tag portable electrical equipment only. It is not a licence to perform electrical installation work.
Can you deliver this course onsite for businesses?
Yes. Group and workplace delivery options are available on request — get in touch and we'll tailor a session for your team.
